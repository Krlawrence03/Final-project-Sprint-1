Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They are used to remember certain information about your visit, such as preferences and usage patterns.

2. How This Site Uses Cookies

kylelawrence.site primarily uses cookies in connection with Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are most popular, or how visitors arrive at the site).

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services such as Google Analytics. These cookies are governed by the privacy policies of those third parties.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage or block cookies through their settings. You can usually find these options in your browser’s privacy or security settings.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
