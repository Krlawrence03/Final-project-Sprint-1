What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your actions and preferences over time.

Cookies Used on This Site

This site primarily uses cookies related to analytics and performance, such as those set by Google Analytics, to understand how visitors use the site.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services (such as Google) may place cookies in your browser to provide analytics or other functionality. These cookies are subject to the third party’s own privacy and cookie policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting them. Note that disabling cookies may affect how some websites function.
