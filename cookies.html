Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember information about your visit.

2. How This Site Uses Cookies

  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics may set cookies to help measure how visitors find and use this site (for example, which pages are most popular and what sources drive traffic).

3. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings where you can block or delete them. If you block cookies, some analytics features may not function as expected, but the site will still be usable.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services (such as Google or LinkedIn) may use their own cookies when you visit linked content or embedded resources. Please review their individual cookie or privacy policies for details.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated over time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
